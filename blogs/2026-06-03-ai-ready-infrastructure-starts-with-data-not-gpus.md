An AI-ready infrastructure strategy starts with the data layer, not the GPU layer. Enterprises invest billions in accelerated compute while training datasets and inference caches remain on storage unsuited for AI access patterns. This mismatch results in idle hardware, stalled pipelines, and missed project targets.
