Managed service providers operate in a structural gap, running Kubernetes platforms their customers depend on while the workloads, clouds, and credentials all belong to the customer. Most enterprise backup tools were not built for this scenario, assuming a centralized operator model with bolt-on multi-tenancy. Portworx Backup 3.0.0 is purpose-built for service providers, addressing the unique operational and ownership challenges of multi-tenant Kubernetes environments.
